Movie-webpage.html

Context

We were given the task of creating a movie website as a group assignment by my teacher, Mr. Mueller. Anna became my new partner, and together we made the decision to create a sequel to the Maze Runner series. The title of this new movie is The Maze Runner: The Kill Order.

Content

Anna and I were required to write an overview of the film. The movie is about a dystopian story set in a virtual world where Thomas, a star character, battles a WICKED spy to destroy the society they created. Using the HTML and CSS knowledge we have gained so far, we had to construct a webpage based on the movie of our choice.

For HTML elements, we needed to include:
  • Heading
  • Paragraphs
  • images
  • links
  • Divs & Spans
  • Google fonts
For CSS elements, we needed to include:
  • Classes
  • IDs
  • Background color
  • Borders
  • Padding
  • Margins

Reflection

Challenges:

Communicating with one another outside of class was one of the challenges I encountered while working on this project. My partner and I had to work on this assignment after school hours in order to complete it on time. It was more difficult to communicate and make your point when we texted each other on Slack. We occasionally fell behind because one of us couldn't be available, but ultimately we met the requirements and completed the job.

Takeaways:
  • Communication and collaboration are essential for a successful partnership.
  • Having patience will help keep a peaceful mindset when completing a big project like this one.
Next Steps:

I'd like to create another movie webpage with a different genre, such as a horror movie. I would also collaborate with my current partner, Anna, because we have similar tastes in movies.
